Who are we?

We are The Anthillers. Get it? Because we all live and work in the Anthill. Well, actually...I guess that would make us Ants. But that’s fucking weird, so we’re The Anthillers.

Marcella Jewell

CEO at Anthill

Marcella is a rad, independent girl building a company all while raising 15 children. Marcella is working on Anthill . “Anthill?”, you say. “That sounds mighty similar to the name of this here institution you’re asking me to donate money too.” Well yes, it is. The Anthill was her idea, and that’s why we named the house after her dog, Anthill. She’s chock full of great ideas. Her grandfather invented the abacus.
