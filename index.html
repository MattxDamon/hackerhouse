Our Philosophy

The philosophy behind the movement: Hard working ants in a concentrated area build magnificent, strong, and intricate little cities. Ants have a division of labor, communication between individuals, an ability to solve complex problems.

Most importantly, they live and work together.

Their ecosystems are emblematic of cities. Anthills represent a whole that is greater than the sum of its parts. Ants are sharers, creators, builders, communicators, and laborers. So are we, and we are ready to work.
