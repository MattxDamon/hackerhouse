The Anthill

The Anthill is great. The Anthill is rad. The Anthill is cool, just like your Dad. 10 of the world’s most average designers, programmers and entrepreneurs — living together, working on amazing projects to help the Tech Valley community.

Who are we?

We are The Anthillers. Get it? Because we all live and work in the Anthill. Well, actually...I guess that would make us Ants. But that’s fucking weird, so we’re The Anthillers.
